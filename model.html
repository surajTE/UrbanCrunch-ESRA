As market volatility and technological disruption impose ever-evolving pressures on businesses, ESRA Augments,  Develops, Upskills and Equips teams with the talent and adaptive mindset necessary to promote and maintain success.

DEVELOP.We will ensure your organizational structure meets your goals. We will develop and bolster your recruitment team to enhance capacity when needed. We will design and enable your Nationalization and inclusivity strategies (including people of determination) and can coach your team to promote cohesion and a winning mindset.

EQUIP.We use the latest technology to support your recruitment pathway. We deliver the right talent, with the right skillsets and the right cultural fit - on time, every time. Through bundled solutions, we will equip your staff and processes for digital age competition.

AUGMENT.We will augment your team at any level across the business, delivering a range of recruitment models: outsourced staffing, volume staffing, permanent placement, recruitment process outsourcing and nationalization, with particular emphasis on the O&G, Energy, Defense, Government and High-tech sectors.

UPSKILLS.We develop the skills your organization, teams and personnel need to adapt to market changes and ever- evolving technological advancement. Options include physical training, capacity building, digital learning online and trade resilience development (upskill and reskill). This includes application of advanced augmented reality technological training solutions.
